Van Helsing Movie

Van Helsing is a 2004 American action horror film directed by Stephen Sommers. It stars Hugh Jackman as Gabriel Van Helsing, a monster hunter tasked by the Vatican to help a beautiful woman, Anna Valerious (Kate Beckinsale), destroy the powerful vampire Count Dracula (Richard Roxburgh). The film is set in the late 19th century Transylvania. Van Helsing is sent by the Vatican to help Anna and her family, who have been tasked for generations to kill Dracula and his brides. Dracula has made a pact with the evil Mr. Hyde, and together they plot to take over the world. Van Helsing teams up with Anna and her gypsy friend Carl to battle Dracula, his brides, and Mr. Hyde. They must use an array of high-tech gadgets and weapons to try to defeat the powerful supernatural forces. Along the way, Van Helsing discovers that he has a mysterious past connected to Dracula. The film features extensive action sequences, special effects, and supernatural elements as Van Helsing fights to stop Dracula's plans and break his family's centuries-old curse. It is a gothic, monster-filled adventure that pits the skilled Van Helsing against the iconic vampire Count Dracula.
