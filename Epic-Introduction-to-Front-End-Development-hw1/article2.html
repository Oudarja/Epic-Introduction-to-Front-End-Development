What is Machine learning?!

Machine learning is a field of artificial intelligence that allows computers to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. It involves developing algorithms and statistical models that enable systems to perform specific tasks effectively without relying on rule-based programming. Machine learning algorithms use data, known as training data, to build models that can make predictions or decisions. These models are then used to analyze new, unseen data and make inferences or predictions about it. The process of training the model on the data and then applying it to new data is at the core of machine learning.
